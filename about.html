About Us

Who We Are

Our journey began with three close friends from high school who were passionate about assisting in Capybara rescue organizations and who also had a dream of starting a modest café. In order to begin our dream business,We diligently saved up money from part-time employment beginning in the year 2019. Our idea was to open a cozy café with a capybara, a place where people could gather, enjoy a moment with the capybaras, and savor delicious coffee and warm pastries. After months of meticulous planning, and searching for the perfect location, Our dream finally became a reality, by the year 2022 we finally got to open our dream cafe along the streets of olive, in Las pinas City, and is now doing a pretty well done job for a small business.
