Welcome to KapeBara !!

Welcome to the enchanting paradise known as "KapeBara Café," where a love of coffee and a capybara's adorable charm unite. Our café offers a one-of-a-kind and amazing experience that pairs the thrill of delighting in delicious meals with the lovely presence of these friendly, soft creatures. 

Our Services

Capy-uccino

This traditional cappuccino is a tasteful combination of espresso, steaming milk, and soft microfoam. It has a strong, intense espresso flavor that is complemented with creamy, foamy milk and is garnished with cocoa or cinnamon.

BaraBerry Cheesecake

This is a blueberry cheesecake. A delectable dish that mixes the creamy, rich cheesecake with the tangy, sweet flavors of blueberries. The crust, which is frequently constructed with a buttery graham cracker or cookie base, offers a solid platform for the decadent layers above.

Capa-Chip

This is a classic blended coffee drink called a Java Chip Frappuccino. It is a delightful blend of creaminess, coffee, and chocolate. A chilled and creamy treat with a perfect blend of flavors and textures. It starts out with a base of freshly brewed coffee, which gives the beverage its rich and powerful coffee flavor.
